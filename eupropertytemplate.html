The Story

A truly remarkable and bespoke triplex loft with emphatic and uncompromising use of steel, glass and wood, to create a fluid and flexible living space. On the top floor of Lofts On The Park, this loft is imbued with the industrial design ethic and truly harks back to the golden age of loft living in the 1990's.

The lower level provides a voluminous open-plan living space with ceiling heights between 4m and 6m and an ever changing lightscape provided by roof lights flooding the vast white walls. Perfect for an art collector or film buff. A super cool metal staircase brings a library into the room and also slightly hides the slick, industrial kitchen under the mezzanine.

The mezzanine provides a wondrous bathroom, dominated by a very cheeky glass bottomed bath. The top floor is just one huge bedroom area and is flooded with natural light via a bank of skylights and features some of the building's heritage detail. A clever glass hatch opens out on to the roof, with both private and communal terraces giving amazing views over Victoria Park and towards the City.

For lovers of industrial loft living and the Hackney lifestyle this is a must see property and extremely competitively priced for such a piece of property art. The location is superb, with one of London's most beloved open spaces, Victoria Park just moments away, with its multitude of brilliant pubs, cafes and restaurants. Just to the east lies Hackney Wick, a vibrant, artsy warehouse district along the River Lee where it meets the London Stadium. Car drivers have a secured off street space and can escape to the Kent and Essex coastal towns with ease via the nearby A12.
